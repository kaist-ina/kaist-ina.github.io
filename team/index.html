Professor

I am a professor at KAIST (Department of Electrical Engineering and Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence). I received a Ph.D. from the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University in 2012. My research interests include networked/cloud systems design, AI for systems, and systems for AI. Jeonghun Lee
Postdoctoral Researcher, Information & Electronics Research Institute

I am a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST INA Lab. I received my Ph.D. in Physics from Simon Fraser University in 2023. My research focuses on developing efficient large language model (LLM) serving systems leveraging distributed networks and consumer-grade GPUs. I aim to make these systems scalable and easily deployable, enabling broader access to advanced AI technologies.
